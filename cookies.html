Cookie Policy

How and why CoachOS uses cookies

Last updated: April 2026  ·  CoachOS (thecoachos.co)

This policy explains what cookies are, which ones CoachOS uses, and how you can control them.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, making it work correctly and improving your experience.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies

These are required for CoachOS to function. Without them you cannot log in or use the platform. They cannot be disabled.

  • sb-auth-token, keeps you logged in to CoachOS (session cookie, expires when you close the browser or after 7 days)
  • sb-refresh-token, refreshes your session securely (7 days)

Preference cookies

These remember your settings so you do not have to re-enter them each visit.

  • coachosLang, remembers your language preference (EN/TR, 1 year)

Analytics cookies

We use anonymised analytics to understand how coaches use the platform and improve it. No personally identifiable data is collected through analytics.

  • Vercel Analytics, page views and performance data, anonymised

We do not use

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party retargeting cookies

Cookies from third parties

If you pay for a subscription, Stripe may set cookies during the payment process. These are governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

How to control cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in. Here's how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome
  • Safari
  • Firefox

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our use of cookies changes. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.
