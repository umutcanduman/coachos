Our story

Built because coaches deserved better tools.

CoachOS started with a simple observation: talented coaches were spending more time on admin than on coaching. Spreadsheets, invoicing apps, booking links, WhatsApp threads, Notion pages. The work that actually matters was being buried under the work of running a practice.


We decided to build the infrastructure that coaches actually need. One place. Purpose-built. Designed around how coaches work, not how software companies think coaches should work.

Our mission

"Give coaches the infrastructure to focus on what they do best: changing people's lives."

Every feature in CoachOS exists to reduce friction and increase the time you spend doing actual coaching. We measure our success by how little you have to think about admin.

What we believe

Built on a few core principles.

🎯

Coaches first

Every decision starts with the coach's experience. Not the investor's pitch, not the feature list. What makes a coach's week better?

🔒

Privacy by design

Client data is sensitive. We take security seriously from the ground up, not as an afterthought. GDPR-compliant, EU-hosted, encrypted end to end.

⚡

Simple over clever

We could build a lot of things. We choose to build fewer things, better. Every feature has to earn its place by making coaches' lives simpler.

🌍

Built for Europe

CoachOS is designed with European coaches in mind: GDPR compliance, EUR pricing, multilanguage support, and EU data residency.

📈

Grow with you

Start with the basics, add what you need. CoachOS scales from your first client to a full coaching studio without changing tools.

🤝

Honest pricing

No per-client fees. No feature gating designed to upsell you. No surprise charges. You know exactly what you're paying and why.
