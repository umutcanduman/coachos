Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: April 2026  ·  CoachOS (thecoachos.co)

CoachOS is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. We operate in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who we are

CoachOS is a software platform built for independent life coaches. Our registered contact is umutcan.duman@gmail.com. When we say "we", "us", or "CoachOS", we mean the platform and its operators.

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash). We never store your password in plain text.

Coaching practice data

As a coach using CoachOS, you may enter data about your clients including their names, contact details, session notes, homework, and payment information. You are the data controller for this information, we process it only on your behalf.

Usage data

We collect anonymised data about how the platform is used, pages visited, features used, and session duration. This helps us improve the product. We do not sell this data.

Payment data

Payments are processed through Stripe. We do not store your full card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve the CoachOS platform
  • To send you product updates and service notifications
  • To process your subscription payments
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

We process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance, to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests, to improve the product and ensure security
  • Legal obligation, where required by law
  • Consent, for marketing communications, where you have opted in

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on servers within the European Union via Supabase (hosted on AWS EU regions). We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access to production data is strictly limited and logged.

6. Client data, coaches as data controllers

If you are a coach, the personal data you enter about your clients (names, contact details, session notes) makes you the data controller for that data under GDPR. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to process that data, informing your clients, and honouring their rights. CoachOS acts as a data processor on your behalf.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at umutcan.duman@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

9. Third-party services

CoachOS uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase, database and authentication
  • Vercel, hosting and delivery
  • Stripe, payment processing

Each has their own privacy policy. We only share data with them to the extent necessary to provide the service.

10. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email. Continued use of CoachOS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at umutcan.duman@gmail.com.
